## Full description (≤4000 chars)
VoltLabs turns your phone into a remote for your VoltLabs smart lamp. Set up your lamp in minutes over Bluetooth, then control it from anywhere your phone has internet — no hub required.

FEATURES

• Power & Brightness — turn your lamp on or off and dial in the exact brightness you want, with live status so you always know if it's online.

• Full Color Control — pick any color from the color wheel, choose from quick presets, or fine-tune warm-to-cool color temperature for the perfect light.

• Scenes & Effects — instantly switch to preset moods like Reading, Relax, Movie, or Night Light, or run dynamic effects like Breathing, Rainbow, and Candle.

• Schedules & Wake-up Light — set daily on/off schedules, a sleep timer that gradually dims before turning off, and a sunrise-simulation wake-up alarm.

• Simple Setup — guided Bluetooth provisioning gets your lamp on your Wi-Fi network in just a few taps.

• Secure Sign-in — sign in with just your phone number via a one-time passcode — no passwords to remember.

• Your Data, Your Control — remove a device or delete your account at any time, right from the app.

Works with VoltLabs smart lamps. Requires Bluetooth and a Wi-Fi network for setup.
